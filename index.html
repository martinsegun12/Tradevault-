What Differentiates a Good Trader from a Bad Trader?

Bad Trader

  • No trading plan or journal
  • Chases losses (revenge trading)
  • Over-leverages with no risk management
  • Blames the market or news
  • Inconsistent strategy hopping
  • Skips post-trade analysis
  • Lets emotions drive decisions

Good Trader

  • Follows a backtested trading plan
  • Accepts losses as part of the game
  • Risk 1-2% per trade max
  • Reviews every trade for improvement
  • Sticks to 1-2 proven strategies
  • Keeps a detailed trading journal
  • Trades with discipline, not emotion
